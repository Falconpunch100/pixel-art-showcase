Falconpunch's Pixel Art Showcase

I've drawn a ton of graphics for 16-bit games for years, and I've improved my technique with each stroke of a brush.
A lot of the stuff I have is old, and I will likely go back and redo them later to see how much I've improved.
Additionally, a few other sets I may show are not drawn by me, due to them being in a collaboration with other graphics artists at the time.
Regardless, I will show you what I have been working on.











This was originally a weird-looking tileset that served only one whole purpose in the game. However, rather than throwing it away, I decided to improve it instead, and add more life to it as well.
There are two palette versions I made for two levels: The first one is for Lava Lift Lair, whilst the other is for Smoldering Shrine.
